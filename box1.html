Type Technical specifications
Size 30, 32, 34,36, 40 ft as per customer demand
Trailer Weight (Unladen) 8-10 Ton (depending on size)
Load Capacity As per Size
Chassis Company fabricated. Formed I Beam - 10 / 10 / 18 – Ladder type construction, Made with high strength steel- with inner cross members providing maximum torsional stability. The Main chassis is a ladder type construction with cross members. The structure ensures maximum load bearing capacity with low Unladen weight Construction also ensures maximum tensional stability & structural rigidity. All steel welded construction. 2 Nos Main longitudinal members are high strength lighter weight Parallel flange Beam running along full length of the trailer
Platform Platform is covered by 4 mm MS sheet or as per customer choice
Side Wall Side Wall 10/12 Gauze TATA steel sheets
Landing Leg Two speed landing leg shall be provided at the front end. (HDTPL/YORK) or plain leg as per customer demand
Axles Axles 12T / 14T / 16T ( YORK/TATA/FUWA/JOST)
King Pin 2” king pin (bolted / Welded). (YORK/JOST)
Suspension Heavy Duty Tridem / Tandem /Tandem + Lifting combo axle. (YORK/TATA)
Brakes Brakes Twin line air brake system with coupled to tractor brake system. Trailer brakes are actuated from tractor. T 24 brake chamber shall be provided on all axles. Parking brake shall be manually actuated. (WEBCO)
Electrical 24 V DC electrical system as per CMVR
Painting Painting Two coats of Epoxy paint shall be provided with Primer
Other Fitment Fitments such SUPD, RUPD, reflectors on sides and rear shall be provided as per CMVR/ ARAI standard guidelines
Wheel Rim 10.00 x 20 – 16 PR – 12 Nos. 7.50 x 20 – 12 Nos
Tyre APPOLO/ JK TYRE/MRF/Others (As per choice)
